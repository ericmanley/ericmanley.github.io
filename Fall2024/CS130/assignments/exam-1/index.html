    Exam 1

    less than 1 minute read

    Due: by the end of the calendar day on Thursday, September 26, 2024

    Complete the test marked Exam 1 on Blackboard.

    This exam is to be completed individually. You may use the book, course slides, or your notes as reference material. You may not use any reference outside of the course materials (including Internet searches and Generative AI). You may not look at the work of other students, whether they are in the class now, have taken it in the past, or have taken a similar course at another school.

    This exam is not timed. You may save your progress and return to finish later, though you may only submit it once.

    The purpose of the free-response questions is for you to demonstrate that you can explain these concepts from your own understanding. A response that has good ideas and is written in your own voice will be valued much more than technically precise sentences copied from the book or some other source.
